Who Am I?

The course so far has been very enjoyable and I would like to give a big thank you to the course creator Andrei Neagoie. I have very little experience of developing websites (as you can probably tell!!!) but so far the course has been brilliant in introducing the key features of html and css and the fact that this page even exists at all shows that I have learned many different skills that I didnt know just a few short days ago.

The creation of an image gallery using flexbox was the point I started to get really hooked on the course and was surprised how easy it was to set this up. I put this down to hard work of Andrei talking us through it in the videos and explaining everything very clearly.
